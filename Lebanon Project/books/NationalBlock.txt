Law enforcement and security in Lebanon is a grey area involving a confluence of actors that include state institutions, international actors and Hezbollah.  The Lebanese Army remains trapped in the inextricable politics of Lebanon, vis-à-vis Hezbollah’s territorial control
Non-state actors have weapons, political clout, and popular support and resist all attempts by the state to disarm them.  Some non-state actors have regional allegiances or are themselves constituted by non-Lebanese members.  Hezbollah is an independent and powerful regional actor that has its own diplomatic and military agenda
